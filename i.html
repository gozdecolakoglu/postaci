Sofitel Legend

Our Sofitel Legend properties are defined by extraordinary storytelling and iconic historical settings. Le Vestiaire brings an additional legendary dimension, elevating the very first impression and naturally sparking conversations with guests about the unique story of each hotel.

To infuse key visible positions with a distinctive historical signature, Sofitel Legend properties benefit from an exclusive customization service. From the doorman to the butler, concierge, reception and guest relations teams, selected silhouettes can be enhanced with bespoke details reflecting the heritage of each destination.

Signature embroideries, emblematic color accents, or historically inspired elements allow every Sofitel Legend to express its singular identity, while preserving the integrity and refined aesthetic of Le Vestiaire. This customization service is available through direct contact with DressBest, the global nominated supplier for Le Vestiaire.

Positions eligible for customization:

  • Doorman / Bellman
  • Concierge
  • Guest Relations
  • Reception
  • Butler

For all other positions, Sofitel Legend properties may select from the Le Vestiaire catalogue.
